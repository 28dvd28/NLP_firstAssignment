Career
Godfrey is a graduate of Presbyterian Ladies' College, Melbourne, where she is remembered as a notale graduate. She was one of four female students at Mr. E. Lenthal Oldfield's Dental College and Oral Hospital where she was a student between 1895 and 1898. She passed the Dental Board exam in November 1898 and registered as a dentist on 8 February 1899.Godfrey practiced dentistry at 34 Collins Street in Melbourne alongside her business partner and future sister-in-law Alys Berry. When she married Dr. Samuel Arthur Ewing in 1903, she stopped her dentistry practice and had three children.
