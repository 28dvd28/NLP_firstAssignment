Ralph Banthorpe (born 6 February 1949) is a retired British international sprinter.

Early life
He lived on Lingfield Avenue in Wolverhampton, and attended
 Wolverhampton Municipal Grammar School (now the Colton Hills Community School). He studied Dentistry at the University of Birmingham.

Career
He competed in the men's 200 metres at the 1968 Summer Olympics.He also represented England in the 200 metres, at the 1970 British Commonwealth Games in Edinburgh, Scotland.
