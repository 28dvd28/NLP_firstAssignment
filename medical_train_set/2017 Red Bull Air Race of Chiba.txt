The 2017 Red Bull Air Race of Chiba was the third round of the 2017 season, the eleventh season of the Red Bull Air Race World Championship. The event was held in Chiba, Japan.

Overview
On January 19, 2017, 8 cities hosting the 2017 season were announced, and following the 2015 season and the 2016 season, it was decided to hold for the third consecutive and the third time in Japan and Chiba prefecture.Following Abu Dhabi of the first game and San Diego of the second game, Chiba of the third game is also a maritime race.
Glay will serve as the ambassador of this event.
Also, Glay also wrote the theme song "XYZ".
