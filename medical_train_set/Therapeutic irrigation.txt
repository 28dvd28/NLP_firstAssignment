In medicine, therapeutic irrigation or lavage ( l…ô-VAHZH or  LAV-ij) is cleaning or rinsing.

Types
Specific types include:

Antiseptic lavage
Bronchoalveolar lavage
Gastric lavage
Peritoneal lavage
Arthroscopic lavage
Ductal lavage
Nasal irrigation
Ear lavage
Pulsed lavage is delivering an irrigant (usually normal saline) under direct pressure that is produced by an electrically powered device, and is useful in cleaning e.g. chronic wounds.
