He served in the United States Marine Corps from 1954 to 1958. Barry was a commercial industrial property manager in Springfield, Virginia. From 1970 to 1983, Barry served in the Virginia House of Delegates, until he resigned when elected Fairfax County Circuit Court Clerk. He then served in the Virginia State Senate from 1991 to 2002. Barry was a Republican.
