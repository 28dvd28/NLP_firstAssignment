Ivan Misner is the founder and Chief Visionary Officer of the business networking organization BNI.

Career
After starting his own consultancy business, Misner founded BNI in Arcadia, California in 1985 after losing an important client at his consultancy. The company expanded through a franchise-based business model.Misner has written several books, including Truth or Delusion? Busting Networking's Biggest Myths and The 29% Solution: 52 Weekly Networking Success Strategies.
