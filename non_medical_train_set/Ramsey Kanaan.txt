Ramsey Kanaan is a Lebanese-Scottish publisher and distributor of anarchist literature. In 1987, he founded AK Press (named after his mother, Ann Kanaan), and later in 2007, of the anarchist publisher PM Press, where he remains the publisher.Kanaan is one of the founders of the Bay Area Anarchist Book Fair and is a member of Bound Together Books in San Francisco, a collectively run anarchist bookstore.In the 1980s he was the singer of the Scottish anarcho-punk band Political Asylum.
