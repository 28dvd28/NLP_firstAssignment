Los Justicieros, "The Avengers", was an anarchist paramilitary group created in 1920 in San Sebasti치n, Basque Region, during the years of pistolerismo. The group was created by Buenaventura Durruti, Suberviola, Ruiz, Aldabatrecu and Marcelino del Campo among others. In 1921, during the inauguration of the Great Kursaal in San Sebastian, members of this group attempted unsuccessfully to assassinate King Alfonso XIII. The group was succeeded by Los Solidarios, another anarchist armed group.
