Uno Laur (born 8 June 1961 in Rakvere), also known as Kohtla-Järve Uno (a nickname derived from his hometown Kohtla-Järve), is an Estonian  anarchist and the iconoclastic ex lead singer of the Must Mamba and Röövel Ööbik, "the oldest punk in Estonia".
