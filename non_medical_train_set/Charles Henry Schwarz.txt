Later, the Toy Bazaar, along with the other brothers' stores, assimilated into FAO Schwarz.  Charles was born in Herford, Province of Westphalia, Kingdom of Prussia and was the first of the Schwarz brothers to immigrate to Baltimore, MD in 1840.
