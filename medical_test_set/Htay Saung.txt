Htay Saung (Burmese: ဌေးဆောင်; born 13 November 1944) is a Burmese dental professor who served as Rector of the University of Dental Medicine, Yangon from 1993 to 1995.

Early life and education
Htay Saung was born in Rangoon, Myanmar on 13 November 1944. He graduated from University of Medicine 1, Yangon in July, 1970. He received Ph.D from Moscow in 1981.
